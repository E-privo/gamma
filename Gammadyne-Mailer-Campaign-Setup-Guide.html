Gammadyne Mailer Campaign Setup Guide

\n
\n
\n

Introduction

\n

This guide will help you set up your email campaigns using Gammadyne Mailer. Follow these steps to ensure your campaigns are successful.

\n
\n
\n

Step 1: Create Your Campaign

\n

Begin by logging into your Gammadyne Mailer account and clicking on the "Create Campaign" button.

\n
\n
\n

Step 2: Design Your Email

\n

Use the built-in template editor to design your email. You can import images and customize the layout.

\n
\n
\n

Step 3: Set Up Your Audience

\n

Select the audience you would like to send the campaign to from your contacts list.

\n
\n
\n

Step 4: Schedule Your Campaign

\n

Choose a date and time for your campaign to go live. Make sure to double-check your settings!

\n
\n
\n

Step 5: Launch!

\n

Click the "Launch" button to start your campaign. Ensure you monitor the results closely.
